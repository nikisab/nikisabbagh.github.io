About Me

With nearly a decade of experience in various administrative and managerial roles, I have honed my skills in supporting top executives, managing operations, and fostering productive business environments. Currently serving as Duty Manager at TOLICA, I oversee daily operations and provide executive support to the CEO, leveraging my expertise gained as a Senior Executive Assistant in the same organization. Prior to this, I excelled as an Executive Assistant to the Managing Director at Doryab Shemiran, where I facilitated seamless communication, managed events, and spearheaded marketing initiatives for new products. My journey in the hospitality industry, highlighted by roles such as Front Desk Supervisor and Housekeeping Supervisor at Homa Hotel Ahmad Abad, instilled in me a strong customer-centric approach and effective team leadership skills. Complementing my practical experience, I hold a Master's degree in IT Management from Ferdowsi University of Mashhad, and a Bachelor's degree in Tourism and Travel Services Management from Binaloud University of Mashhad. With a passion for excellence and a commitment to continuous learning, I am poised to drive organizational success and contribute meaningfully to any team I join.
