About Me

I am an undergraduate student currently in the final year of a four-year B.Sc. degree in Computer Science with an Industrial Placement at Newcastle University. As part of my course I've completed a year-long research placement in Dr. Lucy Collinson’s group in the Electron Microscopy Science Technology Platform at the Francis Crick Institute in London. Working under the supervision of Dr. Martin Jones, I developed bioimage analysis plugins for napari that use machine learning techniques to automate the targeted analysis and segmentation of mitochondrial cristae in volume electron microscopy images. Summer 2023 I also had the opportunity to do a 6-Week research placement in Professor Samra Turajlic's lab, under the supervision of Dr. Charlotte Spencer and Dr. Anne-Laure Cattin Arrighi. Here I was developing a pipeline which would extract statistics relating to the interactivity of different cells in the tumour micro-environment of clear cell renal cell carcinoma biopsies.

The Society of Electron Microscope Technology (SEMT) 2022: RMS Early Career Speakers' Competition
